About Gergana Nikolova

With over 10 years of experience in the wine industry, Gergana Nikolova is a certified sommelier with a passion for helping others discover the world of fine wines. Her expertise spans across European and international wine regions, with a particular focus on Old World wines and their cultural significance.

Expertise & Certifications

  • Certified Sommelier - International Wine Sommelier Association
  • Specialized in wine pairing and food matching
  • Deep knowledge of European wine regions
  • Experience in wine selection and collection management
  • Professional wine tasting and evaluation

My Philosophy

Wine is not just a beverage; it's a story. Each bottle tells a tale of tradition, terroir, and craftsmanship. My mission is to help you understand and appreciate these stories, whether you're a wine enthusiast or just beginning your journey into the world of wines.

I believe that everyone can enjoy and understand wine. There are no rules, only guidelines, and the most important thing is that you enjoy what you drink.
